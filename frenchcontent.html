🤝 French Introductions

▼
Instructions: Learn common French introduction phrases. Each example shows French first, then English translation.

Introducing Yourself

Je m'appelle Marie.
My name is Marie.
Je suis de France.
I am from France.
Je suis professeur.
I am a teacher.

Asking for Names

Comment vous appelez-vous?
What is your name? (formal)
Quel est votre nom?
What is your name?
Tu t'appelles comment?
What's your name? (informal)

Talking About Origin

D'où venez-vous?
Where are you from?
Je viens du Canada.
I come from Canada.
Je suis américain(e).
I am American.

Talking About Work

Que faites-vous?
What do you do?
Je suis médecin.
I am a doctor.
Je travaille dans le marketing.
I work in marketing.
