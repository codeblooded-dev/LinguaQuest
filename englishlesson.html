👋 Greetings & Salutations

▼

Hello / Hi

Universal greeting for any time of day

Pronunciation: /həˈloʊ/ /haɪ/

Good Morning

Used from sunrise until noon

Pronunciation: /ɡʊd ˈmɔrnɪŋ/

Good Afternoon

Used from noon until evening

Pronunciation: /ɡʊd ˌæftərˈnun/

Good Evening

Used from late afternoon until night

Pronunciation: /ɡʊd ˈivnɪŋ/

How are you?

Common way to ask about someone's well-being

Pronunciation: /haʊ ɑr ju/

Nice to meet you

Said when meeting someone for the first time

Pronunciation: /naɪs tu mit ju/

🤝 Introductions

▼

Introducing Yourself

"Hi, I'm Sarah. I'm from Canada."
"Hello, my name is John. Nice to meet you."
"I'm Maria. I work as a teacher."

Asking for Names

"What's your name?"
"May I ask your name?"
"Could you tell me your name?"

Talking About Origin

"Where are you from?"
"I'm from Japan."
"I come from Brazil."

Talking About Work

"What do you do?"
"I'm a doctor."
"I work in marketing."
