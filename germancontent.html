👋 German Greetings & Salutations

▼
Instructions: Click the play buttons to hear German pronunciation. Practice saying each greeting out loud!

Basic Greeting

Hallo
Hello / Hi
Pronunciation: /HAH-loh/

Good Morning

Guten Morgen
Good morning
Pronunciation: /GOO-ten MOR-gen/

Good Day

Guten Tag
Good day / Hello
Pronunciation: /GOO-ten TAHK/

Good Evening

Guten Abend
Good evening
Pronunciation: /GOO-ten AH-bent/

How are you?

Wie geht es Ihnen?
How are you? (formal)
Pronunciation: /vee gayt es EE-nen/

Nice to meet you

Freut mich
Nice to meet you
Pronunciation: /froyt mikh/

🤝 German Introductions

▼
Instructions: Learn common German introduction phrases. Each example shows German first, then English translation.

Introducing Yourself

Ich heiße Anna.
My name is Anna.
Ich komme aus Deutschland.
I come from Germany.
Ich bin Lehrerin.
I am a teacher.

Asking for Names

Wie heißen Sie?
What is your name? (formal)
Wie ist Ihr Name?
What is your name?
Wie heißt du?
What's your name? (informal)

Talking About Origin

Woher kommen Sie?
Where are you from?
Ich komme aus Österreich.
I come from Austria.
Ich bin Amerikaner.
I am American.

Talking About Work

Was machen Sie beruflich?
What do you do for work?
Ich bin Arzt.
I am a doctor.
Ich arbeite im Marketing.
I work in marketing.
